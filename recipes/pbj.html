Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Home

A delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich. An easy favorite!

Description

I don't think that this classic sandwich needs an introduction. Cheap and filling, this is sure to leave you satisfied for lunch. Pair with a banana or an apple for maximum effect.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons of crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons jelly of your choice

Steps

  1. Place both slices of bread face down on a plate.
  2. Using a knife or spoon, slather one face of one piece of bread with peanut butter and the other with jelly.
  3. Assemble the two pieces of bread.
