A plate of homemade Mexican lasagna. Mm-mm!

Description

My family has been making this dish for longer than I've been alive. Not really Mexican nor truly lasagna, it sits in the strange middleground of both. It's delicious, albeit almost certainly not healthy for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of shell noodles
  • 4 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large cans of chili (beans optional)
  • Addons! Black olives go very well with this dish.

Steps

  1. Cook shell noodles in boiling water.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 F
  3. In a baking pan lined with tinfoil, add ingredients. Layer them appropriately to create a faux-lasagna feel to them.
  4. Cook for forty minutes or until the top of the cheese begins to brown at the edges.
